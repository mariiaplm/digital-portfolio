About Me

With the help of great faculty, I've been immersing myself in these concepts and building a love for tech that I know will carry me far.

I enjoy building functional, interactive projects, and I try to create things that are close to what I'm currently learning. I've taken introductory courses in C, Python, and JavaScript, along with intermediate courses in C and JavaScript, and I'm currently taking Algorithms, Software Engineering, and Reinforcement Learning, all of which strengthen the work I do outside the classroom.

I've worked across platforms including Mac and Linux, I'm comfortable navigating GitHub, and I pick up new tools quickly. I work well both independently and as part of a team, and I've taken part in several hackathons hosted by UMass, including a regional one, where I had a lot of fun building under pressure. I try to stay consistent and stay connected to the tech community through the many student-run clubs on campus, and I'm excited to see where this journey takes me.
